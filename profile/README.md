## About Us

Vayusoft Labs is a deep-tech company engaged in **Extreme Computing**, dedicated to making Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations fast, reliable, and accessible.

Incorporated in 2024 as a start-up from **IIT Kanpur**, we utilize AI-native, GPU-enabled CFD softwares to deliver high-fidelity results at a fraction of the computational cost of legacy solvers. Our proprietary technology enables rapid design iterations for industries including Aerospace, Energy, EVs, and Climate-Tech.

We address the high cost and slowness of legacy CFD through a vertically integrated approach:

* **10-100x Faster Simulations:** Leveraging GPU acceleration and pseudo-spectral methods to drastically reduce time-to-solution.
* **AI-Native CFD Engine:** Integrating machine learning with traditional physics solvers for enhanced turbulence diagnostics and speed.
* **Hybrid Cloud Scalability:** Seamlessly scalable across GPU clusters and multi-CPU systems, available via a "Pay-as-you-use" cloud service.
* **Patent-Protected:** Backed by unique software-hardware patent.

## Product Ecosystem

Our platform consists of specialized solvers and tools catering to different flow regimes, ranging from TRL-5 to TRL-8.

### 1. TARANG
* **Type:** Massively Parallel Pseudo-Spectral Solver.
* **Capabilities:** Hydrodynamic Turbulence, Magnetohydrodynamics (MHD), Rayleigh-BÃ©nard Convection, Rotating Flows.
* **Tech Stack:** CPU/GPU hybrid support (CUDA-accelerated).

### 2. AITHON
* **Type:** General Purpose Hydro-Astrophysical Spectral Solver.
* **Capabilities:** Ideal for fundamental fluid dynamics and astrophysical simulations.
* **Tech Stack:** High-performance heterogeneous compute support, scaled on the fastest supercomputers.

### 3. DHARA
* **Type:** General Purpose ODE Solver.
* **Capabilities:** Finite-difference/Finite-Volume cores designed for complex compressible flows.
* **Tech Stack:** CPU/GPU hybrid support (CUDA-accelerated).

### 4. PRAVAH
* **Type:** High-order Navier-Stokes Solver.
* **Capabilities:** Targets Turbulent flows, including Large Eddy Simulation (LES) to study hypersonic flows.

### 5. Turbulence Diagnostics
* **Type:** Deep Flow Analysis Tool.
* **Capabilities:** AI-driven flow breakdown metrics and visual structure identification to enhance design tuning.
